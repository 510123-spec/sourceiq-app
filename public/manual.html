1. The daily routine

SourceIQ works best as a morning habit:

  1. Open the dashboard — on this PC: http://localhost:3001 · from any office device: http://192.168.1.109:3001
  2. Check 🔔 Leads — a number badge means new suppliers, buyers, or buy requests were found overnight for your watched searches. Open it, review only what's new.
  3. Star the good ones — ☆ on any result adds it to the shared shortlist (a trust check runs automatically in the background).
  4. Check ⭐ Saved — a red badge means a deal has been sitting 7+ days without progress. Chase it or close it.
  5. Work your searches — anything the leads didn't cover, search manually in the tabs.

5. The Saved shortlist — your pipeline

Open with ⭐ Saved. Every saved company has:

  • Pipeline status — click the stage: 🆕 New → 📨 Contacted → 💰 Quoted → 📦 Sampled → ✅ Ordered (or 🚫 Rejected). Keep this updated — it's the team's single source of truth.
  • ⏱ Age badge — how long it has sat in the current stage. Amber at 7 days, red at 14: follow up or close.
  • Notes — free text, saved automatically, visible to the whole team. Write quotes, impressions, next steps.
  • 💹 Deal math — enter Buy, Freight, Duties, Sell (per MT/unit) and see the margin and % instantly. Shared with the team.
  • 🛡 Trust badge — the automatic check's verdict (green/amber/red).
  • 📊 Export — the whole shortlist as an Excel file: statuses, contacts, trust, notes, margins. Your weekly sourcing report.

6. The Lead Monitor

  1. Run a Product or Buyers search you care about.
  2. Click 🔔 Watch (in the toolbar above product results, or the buyers header). Up to 10 watched searches.
  3. Every morning (after 7:00) the system re-runs all watches by itself and remembers everything it has ever shown you.
  4. The 🔔 badge shows how many genuinely new results appeared. Open the panel to see them — star the good ones directly.
💡 The first run of a new watch shows "baseline captured" — new items start appearing from the next day. Use ▶ Run Now in the panel to check on demand.

7. The 🧠 Copilot

The blue brain button (bottom-right) opens a chat assistant that can actually use the system for you — it searches, checks trust, reads your pipeline, and saves companies. Small chips under each answer show what it did. Things to ask:

  • "Which of my deals are going stale?"
  • "Find aluminium scrap buyers in India and save the best two."
  • "What margin am I making on my quoted deals?"
  • "Search copper cathode suppliers in Chile, run trust checks, and tell me which to contact first."
  • "Draft a follow-up email for the supplier that quoted 12 days ago."
💡 The Copilot only uses real data from searches and your shortlist — it is instructed never to invent companies, prices, or contacts. Complex requests can take up to a minute.

8. Live market prices

The strip under the top bar shows the metals you trade:

  • Copper, Gold, Silver — live market prices (futures), refreshed every 15 minutes, with the recent change so you see the direction. Copper also shows the converted USD/MT figure traders quote in.
  • Tin & Antimony — these minor metals have no free live feed, so the bar keeps a reference price you maintain yourself: click the ✏ to update it whenever you have a fresh market quote. It's clearly marked as your reference, not a live price.
💡 Use it before quoting: if copper moved 3% since a supplier's offer, that offer may be stale — renegotiate.

9. Marketing tools

📣 Marketing Kit (top bar)

Enter a product you sell plus only TRUE details (specs, quantities, terms). The AI writes: a portal listing (title + body) ready for TradeWheel / Go4WorldBusiness / Alibaba, a LinkedIn post, an outreach email, the HS code, and the keywords buyers search. Copy each section, export all to Word, or jump straight to the portals' posting pages. Unknown specs become [placeholders] — it never invents claims.

📣 Outreach Campaign (inside ⭐ Saved)

Pick up to 15 shortlisted companies and the product you're offering. The AI writes one personalized email per company (it uses their type, country, and your notes). Review each, open in your email app, or export the whole batch to Excel for mail-merge. You press send — the system never emails anyone by itself.

10. Your product catalog

A professional product page you can share with any customer:

  1. Click 🗂 Catalog → add your products (name, description, specs, terms).
  2. Share the public page: http://192.168.1.109:3001/catalog.html (works for anyone on the office network; customers visiting your office can scan/open it too).
  3. Visitors can send a quote request from the page — it lands back in your 🗂 Catalog inbox, and the badge shows unread requests.
💡 The Company Brain also works for you here: search results show a 🧠 Known badge on any company the system has dealt with before — hover it to see your history with them. You can also ask the Copilot: "Have we dealt with codelco.com before?"

12. Troubleshooting

ProblemWhat to do
Page looks old / a button is missingPress Ctrl+F5 (hard refresh).
SEARCH gauge red ("Down")The Brave Search subscription has a billing problem — check api-dashboard.search.brave.com. Searches still work, with reduced quality, until fixed.
AI gauge red / AI features stopGemini quota or billing issue — check aistudio.google.com. Everything non-AI keeps working.
Site not reachable at allThe server self-heals within 2 minutes (a watchdog restarts it). If the PC was off, just turn it on and wait a minute. Colleagues must be on the office network.
Search finds few resultsTry broader words ("copper cathode" not "grade A copper cathode 99.99% LME"), or remove the country filter. Check the SEARCH gauge is green.
Results look wrong for the countryThe note above results explains when the country filter couldn't verify matches — treat those results with care.
⚠️ Good practice: details scraped from the web (prices, addresses, contacts) are a starting point — always verify critical facts directly with the company before ordering. The trust check is a signal, not a guarantee.
